About Us

Elite Business Solution (EBS) established in 2014. EBS vision is a vision shared by its owners, a team of IT specialists who have been working in the field for the past 15 years.
Our Vision is to be one of the market leader in the field of enterprise resource planning software & to help every business organization to achieve its goals using our set of solutions.
Our Team each one of our team member is working with full commitment and passion towards our client he/she works with. This has been and will always be the case.
Our Mission as we are a Microsoft Dynamics Partner with Solid Experience in ERP & CRM Products we provide our customer with a high level of Professional service in both Microsoft Dynamics Products and our owned solutions.

Business consultation

Through the expertise of our professional team whose skills encompass a variety of business processes, we offer consultancy services to clients who seek guidance as far as the operation of these processes is concerned.

Process re-engineering

By virtue of being pioneers in different business processes, we help our clients re-think the traditional strategies with which they operate their businesses in order to arrive at the best performance standards.

Change management & training services

Our professional staff provides Functional, technical and Development training services as well as operational and management cycle refinement programs.  We provide a structured approach to shifting / transitioning individuals, teams, and organizations from a current state to a desired future state

Maintenance & Support Service

Customers are offered a high level support service through specialized staff whose members provide accurate consultation and prompt assistance. Through our after-sales service, which covers system optimization, system upgrade, etc., we make sure that all the client’s problems are resolved in due time and whenever needed.
